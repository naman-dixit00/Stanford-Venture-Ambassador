# Stanford Venture Fellowship | Ambassador Engagement

## Overview
The Stanford Venture Fellowship connects ambitious students with founders, builders, and researchers within the Stanford and Silicon Valley ecosystem. This repository documents the strategic outreach, community engagement, and global network expansion efforts associated with the Ambassador role. 

Ambassadors become part of a global network of individuals building companies, technologies, and institutions that shape the future. As the fellowship grows beyond its flagship one-week program, ambassadors help build and lead that network across continents.

## The Ambassador Role
[cite_start]In addition to fellows, the Stanford Venture Fellowship invites a very small number of individuals each year to serve as Ambassadors[cite: 11]. 

* [cite_start]**Structural Position:** Ambassadors sit above fellows and help guide the global expansion of the program[cite: 12].
* **Representation:** They represent the fellowship within their respective schools, regions, and international communities.
* [cite_start]**Objective:** A primary responsibility is to surface and introduce exceptional individuals who should be integrated into the ecosystem[cite: 15, 16].

## Selection & Community Integration
[cite_start]Ambassador invitations are extended to a very small number of individuals representing their schools and countries[cite: 17]. The selection is based on demonstrated initiative, leadership, and the proven ability to connect exceptional communities.
